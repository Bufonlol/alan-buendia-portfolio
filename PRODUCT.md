# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, engineering leads, studios, and potential clients evaluating Alan Buendía's frontend engineering work. They need to understand his capabilities, shipped projects, technical range, and availability quickly on desktop or mobile.

## Product Purpose

Present Alan's work as credible evidence of frontend engineering ability and make it easy to inspect projects or start a conversation. Success means visitors can identify his role, strongest work, stack, location, and contact path without waiting for decorative effects.

## Brand Personality

Precise, technical, and restless. The experience should feel like a living technical document: confident and memorable, but never obscure or slow.

## Anti-references

No cyberpunk, neon, glassmorphism, gaming UI, generic SaaS cards, decorative WebGL, excessive smooth scrolling, or technical noise that competes with information.

## Design Principles

1. Information wins over effects.
2. Motion explains hierarchy and never delays access.
3. Technical character comes from typography, structure, and real project evidence.
4. Every interaction should feel immediate.
5. Mobile and reduced-motion experiences are first-class.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and keyboard support. Respect reduced motion, retain semantic text and landmarks, provide visible focus states, and avoid interactions that require a fine pointer.
